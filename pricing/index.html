FAQ

Common questions.

What's included in the $2,500 setup fee?

Everything needed to go from zero to a live assistant: discovery consultation, a dedicated Mac mini M4 configured and shipped to you, full software installation, your custom knowledge base, channel integrations, automated workflows, and 2 months of Pro-level support. After those 2 months you move to Basic or Pro.

What's the difference between Basic and Pro?

Basic keeps your assistant running and up to date — software updates, security patches, and monitoring. Pro adds active optimization: new skills, workflow improvements, LLM tuning, and 2 hours of hands-on support each month. Most clients start on Pro and drop to Basic once they're dialed in.

When would I use the hourly rate?

For larger projects outside the monthly scope — building a custom integration, setting up a new data source, on-site configuration, or a significant workflow rebuild. Billed in 15-minute increments at $125/hr. Remote work is standard; on-site is the same rate plus travel.

What are API credits and what do they cost?

Your assistant uses Claude by Anthropic to think and respond. You pay Anthropic directly — typically $50–150/month depending on usage. We help you set up your account. This is separate from the monthly service fee.

Who owns the hardware?

You do. The Mac mini is shipped to you and it's yours permanently. Your data lives on your machine. We configure and maintain it remotely — we don't have access to your conversations.

Can I cancel?

Yes, any time. Monthly billing only — no contracts. Your assistant keeps running as long as you have Anthropic credits. The Mac mini stays with you.
